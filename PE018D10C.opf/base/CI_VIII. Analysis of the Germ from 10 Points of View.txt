yathā śāriputra pradīpaḥ | avinirbhāgadharmā | avinirmuktaguṇaḥ | yad uta ālokoṣṇavarṇatābhiḥ

ślokaḥ |

|| 47 ||anena kiṃ darśitam |svabhāvādibhir ity ebhiḥ ṣaḍbhir arthaiḥ samāsataḥ |dhātus

iti ye kecid anāsravadhātunirdeśā nānādharmaparyāyamukheṣu bhagavatā vistareṇa nirdiṣṭāḥ sarve

balatām adhigatas tathāgato ’rhan samyaksaṃbuddha ity ucyate |

iva || 50 ||
